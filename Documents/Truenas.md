## What is a NAS?

A NAS is like cloud storage but instead of being in Icloud or google drive, its a decicated device designed to store data and often for very long periods of time. The way you access your data is though a network storage protocol such as [syncthing](https://syncthing.net/). 




## Prerequisites

- An old laptop or desktop
> [!IMPORTANT]
> the boot drive **CAN'T** be used as extra storage
- Storage drive that can connect to the laptop
